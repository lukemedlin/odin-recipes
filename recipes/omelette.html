Omelette

Description

This is an omelette recipe created by chatgpt

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: diced vegetables, diced ham or bacon, shredded cheese, etc.

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them together with a fork or whisk. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat. Once the pan is hot, add the butter or oil.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and let them cook undisturbed for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add any desired fillings to one half of the omelette, then use a spatula to fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings.
  5. Cook for another minute or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted (if using).
  6. Use the spatula to carefully slide the omelette onto a plate.
  7. Serve immediately.
