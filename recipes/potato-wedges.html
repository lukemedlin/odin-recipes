Potato Wedges

Description

I found this recipe after searching for potato wedges recipes from reddit. It was contained in the second link, and had a few upvotes

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut potatos into wedges.
  2. Soak in water for 10 minutes
  3. Place onto baking pan that is covered with parchment paper
  4. Cook in oven at 400 degrees Farenheit for 55 minutes
